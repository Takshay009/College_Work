Naruto

*Naruto* is a Japanese manga and anime about Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the strongest leader, or Hokage, of his village. Despite being shunned for hosting a powerful fox spirit, Naruto trains hard, makes friends, and overcomes challenges, embodying themes of perseverance and friendship.

Sasuke

Sasuke Uchiha is a skilled, determined ninja in *Naruto* who seeks revenge on his older brother, Itachi, for destroying their clan. Initially a teammate of Naruto, Sasuke leaves the village to gain power, driven by his desire for vengeance. His journey explores themes of loyalty, ambition, and redemption.
